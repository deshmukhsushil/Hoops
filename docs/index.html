Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for job seekers who are serious about their search. Hoops handles the busywork so you can focus on the interview.

⚡️

Instant Question Extraction

Hoops automatically detects mandatory subjective questions on Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, Ashby, SmartRecruiters, and more the moment the page loads.

📄

Resume Parser v2.0

Upload your PDF or DOCX resume once. Hoops extracts the text locally, strips PII optionally, and stores it — ready to inject into every prompt.

📊

Job Tracker

Every saved application lands in a clean dashboard with date, company, role, job description, and the exact resume version used. Never lose track again.

📋

1-Click JD Copy v2.0

The extension scrapes the full job description from Lever, Greenhouse, and others and puts it in your clipboard with one click — no manual selecting needed.

🔒

Auto PII Removal v2.0

Your email, phone number, address, and social links are automatically stripped from stored resume text. Your data stays clean and private.

💾

Auto-Save on Submit v2.0

Toggle auto-save on and Hoops will automatically log the job, description, and resume used the moment you hit submit on an application form.

🧠

Smart Context

Paste your resume or bio once. Every extracted question panel includes your context baked into the copy prompt — so AI writes answers that sound like you.

🛡️

100% Local. Zero Upload.

Everything — parsing, storage, scraping — happens on your device. No accounts, no servers, no telemetry. Your data never leaves your browser.

Changelog

What's new in v2.0

A major upgrade focused on security, intelligence, and reliability.

🔐

Full Security Hardening

Rewrote all input sanitization using strict regex escaping to eliminate XSS attack vectors. Added URL protocol validation to prevent javascript: URI injection.

📄

Resume PDF & DOCX Parsing

Upload your actual resume file. Hoops extracts text entirely in-browser using PDF.js and Mammoth.js — no file ever leaves your device.

🧹

Auto PII Scrubbing

Emails, phone numbers, addresses, and social URLs are detected and removed from stored resume text automatically when the setting is enabled.

📋

Job Description Extraction

Hoops now fetches the full job description from Lever and other ATS platforms via their public APIs and lets you copy it to clipboard in one click.

💾

Auto-Save on Application Submit

A new toggle on the dashboard lets Hoops automatically log a job — with description and resume version — the moment you submit an application form.

🐛

Bug Fixes: Date & Deduplication

Fixed invalid date storage, eliminated duplicate job entries, and cleaned up company name extraction across all supported ATS platforms.
