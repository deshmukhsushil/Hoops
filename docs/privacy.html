Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Hoops ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

Hoops operates entirely locally on your device. When you use the extension to extract questions from a job application page, the processing happens within your browser. No data is sent to our servers or any third parties.

2. Permissions

The extension requires specific permissions to function:

  • activeTab: To read the content of the current job application page you are viewing.
  • clipboardWrite: To allow you to copy the extracted questions to your clipboard.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support page.
